Advanced Statistics Glossary

  • AST/TO - Assist to Turnover Ratio
  • AST% - Percentage of teammate field goals the player assisted while on court
  • REB% - Percentage of available rebounds the player grabbed while on court
  • TOV% - Percentage of Turnovers by a player against all player actions
  • PIE - Player Impact Estimate: A measure of a player's overall statistical contribution
  • eFG% - Effective Field Goal Percentage: Adjusts for 3-pointers being worth more
  • TS% - True Shooting Percentage: Accounts for 2-pointers, 3-pointers, and free throws
  • OFFRTG - Points scored per 100 possessions while player was on court
  • DEFRTG - Points allowed per 100 possessions while player was on court
  • NETRTG - Difference between offensive and defensive rating
  • TO/100 - Team turnovers per 100 possessions while player/lineup was on court

*All possession-based statistics are estimates based on recorded statistical events

*All player minutes are estimates based on real time entries of tip off, period start/end, and substitution entries
