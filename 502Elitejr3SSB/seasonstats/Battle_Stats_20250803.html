Advanced Statistics Glossary

  • AST/TO - Assist to Turnover Ratio
  • AST% - Percentage of teammate field goals the player assisted while on court
  • REB% - Percentage of available rebounds the player grabbed while on court
  • TOV% - Percentage of Turnovers by a player against all player actions
  • PIE - Player Impact Estimate: A measure of a player's overall statistical contribution. Formula: (PTS + FGM + FTM - FGA - FTA + DREB + OREB + AST + STL + BLK - PF - TO) ÷ (Game Total of Same Stats) × 100
  • eFG% - Effective Field Goal Percentage: Adjusts for 3-pointers being worth more. Formula: (FGM + 0.5 × 3PM) ÷ FGA
  • TS% - True Shooting Percentage: Complete shooting efficiency including free throws. Formula: Points ÷ (2 × (FGA + 0.44 × FTA))
  • OFFRTG - Offensive Rating: Points scored per 100 possessions while player was on court
  • DEFRTG - Defensive Rating: Points allowed per 100 possessions while player was on court
  • NETRTG - Net Rating: Difference between offensive and defensive rating
  • TO/100 - Team turnovers per 100 possessions while player/lineup was on court
  • 2nd CH - Second Chance Points: Points scored immediately after your team gets an offensive rebound
  • PITP - Points in the Paint: Points scored from shots taken within 6 feet of the basket

*All possession-based statistics are estimates based on recorded statistical events

*All player minutes are estimates based on real time entries of tip off, period start/end, and substitution entries
